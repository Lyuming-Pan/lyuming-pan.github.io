Topic Speech on
University Orientation

In September 2020, I was invited to give a topic speech to all the new graduate students of Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School in the university orientation week. As the president of the Advisory Committee of Graduate Students, my topic speech focuses on three aspects: sharing learning and research experience, participating in university education reform, and organizing students to communicate with teachers. The purpose of this topic speech is to help new graduate students participate more in the teaching, scientific research and future development planning of Tsinghua University, so as to create a better university education.

Being A Teaching Assistant

From September 2020 to January 2021, I worked as a teaching assistant of modern sensing technology, which is a professional required course for graduate students of instrument engineering. In this course, I am responsible for about one third of the class hours - experimental courses. I have completed six experimental courses, including experimental design, experimental system construction and part of experimental teaching.
