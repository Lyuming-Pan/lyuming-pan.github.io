In Energy System

The energy problem is becoming an important challenge for mankind.
In September 2020, at the 75th General Assembly of the United Nations, President Xi Jinping pointed out that China plans to reach the peak carbon dioxide emissions by 2030 and strive to achieve carbon neutrality by 2060.
In December 2020, The U.S. Department of Energy released the Energy Storage Grand Challenge Roadmap, which is the first comprehensive energy storage strategy of the United States.
In these sustainable energy planning, redox flow batteries, lithium metal batteries and fuel cells are regarded as potential energy systems.
In the future, we need more outstanding energy system engineers, which will be the role I strive to be.
